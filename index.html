Shopping flow

How the shopping flow works

Step 01

Register

The customer downloads the app, creates an account, adds a payment method, and completes identity setup using facial recognition or QR-based access.

Step 02

Enter

The customer unlocks the store or cabinet zone through a verified entry flow. The session opens automatically in the platform.

Step 03

Pick products

Smart shelves and cabinets track product interactions using weight sensing, cabinet logic, camera verification, and inventory controls.

Step 04

Exit

When the customer leaves, the system closes the session, reconciles the basket, and processes payment automatically.

Step 05

Receive receipt

A digital receipt is sent instantly in the app, while the platform updates stock, supplier analytics, and replenishment triggers.

Hardware

A modular cabinet system built for real-world retail

Each Micro Shop location is assembled from a network of specialized cabinets that can be configured for different product categories and operating conditions. The format supports refrigerated goods, packaged items, ready-to-eat meals, and category-specific storage and access logic.

A typical location can be configured from 6 to 8 cabinets depending on the site, category mix, and traffic profile.

  • Refrigerated

    Cabinets for dairy and chilled packaged goods

  • Controlled

    Cabinets for meat trays and sensitive perishables

  • Ambient

    Smart ambient cabinets for everyday packaged products

  • Ready-meal

    Modules with heating or preparation workflows

  • Access control

    Entry and exit control points for secure autonomous operation

Technology
